# Check, Please! Philly — Episode Guide

*Food and travel writer Kae Lani Palmisano invites local diners to join in the conversation about great restaurants in the Philadelphia area. Each week, three regular people – not professional restaurant critics – recommend their favorite spot to eat out.*
